Independent christian network

 
 
 
 
 

Located in beautiful San Francisco, Independent Christian Network offers congregants a place of comfort, hope, and a peace of mind for all their worshipping needs. Our mission is to ordain exceptionally knowledgeable ministers of the Gospel to go out into the world to establish a network of independent churches and ministries. ICN is non-denominational and we believe the Holy Bible is the only truth, so we don't adopt doctrines of various denominations that fail to be proven in the Word of God. We are dedicated to spreading the teachings of Jesus Christ, and our goal is to inspire you through the holiness of our dwelling and our services. We invite you to become a part of our growing congregation, and look forward to meeting you!
